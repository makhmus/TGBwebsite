Why FiveM RP Rules Matter

Roleplay rules exist to maintain realism, fairness, and immersive storytelling.

Common FiveM RP Rules

Fail RP

Acting unrealistically or ignoring consequences breaks immersion.

Metagaming

Using out-of-character information in roleplay situations is prohibited.

Powergaming

Forcing unrealistic actions or outcomes onto other players is not allowed.
